FLOWERS

Fresh flowers are recently cut or picked blooms that are still vibrant, fragrant, and haven't started to wilt or wither. They are characterized by firm stems and leaves, moist stem ends, and a natural fragranceThey are visually appealing, often used for decoration and gifting, and convey emotions and sentiments. Fresh flowers differ from dried or artificial ones by their natural beauty and unparalleled fragrance.
